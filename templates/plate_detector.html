How It Works
  1. Upload an image containing a license plate
  2. Select whether it's an Entry or Exit detection
  3. System automatically detects and crops the plate
  4. OCR recognizes characters in the plate
  5. Review the detected text below the cropped image
  6. Edit manually if needed in the correction box
  7. Click "Save to Database" to store the plate
Tip: For best results, ensure the license plate is clearly visible and well-lit in the image.
